# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to uncover insights related to revenue, profit, product performance, and regional trends.

The dashboard is designed to help businesses make data-driven decisions by identifying key growth opportunities and performance gaps.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze sales and profit trends over time  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Evaluate regional and segment-wise performance  
- Highlight loss-making areas  
- Provide actionable business recommendations  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Handling)
- GitHub (Project Documentation)

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- Contains: Orders, Sales, Profit, Category, Region, Segment, etc.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Sales: 2.30M  
- Total Profit: 286.40K  
- Total Orders: 5K  
- Total Quantity: 38K  
- Profit Margin: 12.47%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales Overview
- KPI cards for business performance
- Sales trend over time
- Regional sales comparison

### 2. Product & Category Analysis
- Top 10 products by sales
- Category-wise sales vs profit
- Sub-category profit distribution

### 3. Region & Segment Analysis
- Profit by region
- Sales by segment
- State-level sales distribution

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- West region generates the highest profit  
- Consumer segment contributes maximum sales  
- Technology category performs best overall  
- Some sub-categories (Tables, Bookcases) are loss-making  
- Sales are concentrated in a few states  

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-profit categories like Technology  
- Reduce discounts on loss-making products  
- Improve strategy in low-performing regions  
- Maintain stock for top-selling products  
- Use data trends for better decision-making
